About

01 · Studio

Jhorro Productions is the independent label behind the work of Jhonnatan Barbosa — a game designer with a statistics background and a habit of iterating with spreadsheets and player data. We make games, prototypes, and interactive experiences, with an emphasis on systems that hold together and mechanics that earn their place.

Small by design, broad by curiosity. From commercial mobile titles to game jams, the throughline is the same: watch closely, measure honestly, and build deliberately.
