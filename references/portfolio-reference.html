Case Study — Idle Kingdom Builder

Economy design · Data-driven iteration · 30% D30 retention
01 · Problem

Players churning at mid-game

Early progression felt satisfying, but analytics showed a drop-off once resource generation plateaued. Players weren't reaching the ascension system.

02 · Process

Spreadsheet modeling + analytics

Built economy simulation spreadsheets to map resource faucets and sinks. Used Unity Analytics to pinpoint exact drop-off points in the progression funnel.

03 · Solution

Rebalanced curves + new sinks

Adjusted progression curves to maintain a sense of growth through mid-game. Added meaningful resource sinks and tuned the ascension unlock timing.

04 · Outcome

30% D30 retention

3–6× the casual mobile average (5–10%). 800+ organic downloads. Lessons directly shaped the expanded economy in Idle Cyberpunk City Clicker.

Process artifacts: economy flow spreadsheets, progression curve charts, Unity Analytics dashboards, before/after balancing data, player funnel screenshots — this is where your statistics background shines.
